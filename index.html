About TRIO

A Legacy of Support Since 1987

TRIO (Transplant Recipients International Organization) was established in the mid-1980s by a group of transplant patients at the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center. Incorporated as a not-for-profit in 1987, TRIO became an international organization in 1994, moving its headquarters to Washington D.C.

TRIO-Oklahoma was founded in 2020 by Lorrinda Gray-Davis, its first President. Jeff Collins currently serves as President. We are a 501(c)(3) non-profit membership organization working to improve the quality of life for transplant recipients, candidates, donor families, and healthcare professionals.

Mission Statement

Team Oklahoma 2024

Team Oklahoma's mission is to demonstrate the success of organ, eye and tissue donation. We serve as a family, to our members, by providing love, support and understanding. We strive to honor the memory of our donors and show gratitude to their family for the gifts given.
